About PawCare

We Treat Every Pet Like Family

Founded in 2009, PawCare was built on a simple belief — every animal deserves compassionate, evidence-based care. Our multidisciplinary team works together to provide the best outcomes for your companions.

  • ✓ Board-certified veterinarians across 8 specialties, available 7 days a week
  • ✓ State-of-the-art in-clinic diagnostics including digital X-ray and ultrasound
  • ✓ Secure online portal to track medical records, reminders, and prescriptions
  • ✓ Multilingual support — Hindi, Marathi, English, and more
  • ✓ Home visit services available across Pune and surrounding areas
